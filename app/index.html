Tic-Tac-Toe

About the game
Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic game. The objective is simple. Your goal is to get three in a row of whichever icon you play as, before your opponent. For this version, that will either be Earth , or Sun .
Winning at Tic-Tac-Toe is usually the result of your opponent making a careless mistake. Computers don't usually make too many mistakes, so I've added three game modes to make the experience a bit more enjoyable.
Easy mode will yield many wins for the user, the computer won't be making too many smart decisions here.
Medium mode will yield many ties and a few wins here and there.
Hard mode will yeild many ties and no wins. Whether it yields you losses or not, depends on how well you play.
Good luck and have fun!
